How to request deletion

Send us a deletion request by email and we’ll remove your account and associated data from our systems.

Email support@goalmindstudios.com from the email address linked to your account in Football Live TV Stream AiGoal.
Use the subject line “Delete my account — Football Live TV Stream AiGoal” so we can find it quickly.
If you signed in without an email, include your in-app User ID (found in the app’s Settings screen) so we can identify your account.
We confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days and email you once it’s done.
✉ Email a deletion request

What gets deleted

When your request is processed, we permanently delete the data tied to your account, including:

  • Your account identifier and sign-in details
  • App preferences and saved settings
  • Push-notification token used to send match alerts
  • In-app activity associated with your account (such as viewing presence)

What we may keep, and for how long

We may retain a limited amount of information where the law requires it or to prevent fraud and abuse — for example, transaction or security records. Such data is kept only as long as legally necessary and is not used to re-create your account. Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot identify you may be retained.

Third-party services used by the app (for example, Google Firebase for app infrastructure and ad partners) process data under their own policies. Deleting your account removes your data from our systems; you can also manage device-level identifiers in your phone’s settings.

Questions

For anything about your account or this process, contact us at support@goalmindstudios.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
