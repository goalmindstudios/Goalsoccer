Delete Your Account & Data

AiGoal Score · by GoalMind Studios

You can request deletion of your AiGoal Score account and all associated data at any time. Follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to goalmindstudiospro@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account (or include your in-app display name if you use Guest mode).
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete my AiGoal Score account".
  3. We will verify the request and permanently delete your account within 7 days.
You can also delete your account directly inside the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account (where available).

What data is deleted

  • Your account identifier (Google or Guest), name, email, and profile photo.
  • Your predictions, statistics (wins, XP, streaks), and leaderboard entries.
  • Your notification token and app activity data.

What data is kept

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics that cannot identify you may be retained.
  • Records we are required to keep for legal or fraud-prevention purposes, for the minimum period required by law.

For any questions, contact us at goalmindstudiospro@gmail.com. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
