This Privacy Policy explains how AiGoal Score ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"), published by Mysticcraftz, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, profile photo, and Google account identifier. If you continue as a guest, we create an anonymous account identifier only (no personal details).
  • App activity — Predictions you create, your statistics (wins, XP, streaks), display name, and chosen avatar, which are stored to power your profile and the leaderboard.
  • Device & usage data — Device model, operating system version, app version, language, general usage events, and crash diagnostics, used to keep the App stable and improve features.
  • Advertising identifiers — Your device's advertising ID and related data may be processed by our advertising partners to deliver and measure ads (see Section 4).
  • Push notification token — If you enable notifications, a device token is used to send match and goal alerts.

We do not collect payment information. The App is free to use.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: live scores, fixtures, standings, AI predictions, and the prediction game.
  • To maintain your profile, statistics, and the community leaderboard.
  • To send optional match and goal notifications.
  • To display advertising that helps keep the App free.
  • To analyze usage, fix crashes, and improve performance.
